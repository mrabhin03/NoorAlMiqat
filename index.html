Our Services

Air-Conditioning, Ventilations & Air Filtration Systems Installation & Maintenance

Our expert team specializes in the installation and maintenance of air conditioning, ventilation, and air filtration systems, ensuring optimal indoor air quality and temperature control.

Electromechanical Equipment Installation and Maintenance

We provide professional installation and maintenance of electromechanical systems, including elevators, escalators, pumps, motors, and generators.

Plumbing & Sanitary Installation

Our plumbing and sanitary installation services cover everything from water supply systems to drainage solutions. We install and maintain pipes, fixtures, and fittings with the highest level of expertise, ensuring efficient water distribution and waste management.

Floor & Wall Tiling Works

We offer top-quality floor and wall tiling services, delivering precise and durable installations for various spaces. Our experienced team handles everything from surface preparation to tile alignment, using high-quality materials to ensure long-lasting results.

Carpentry & Wood Flooring Works

Our carpentry and wood flooring services bring craftsmanship and quality to your spaces. We install and repair wood flooring, and create bespoke cabinetry, doors, window frames, and other wooden elements tailored to your specifications.

Engraving & Ornamentation Works

We provide specialized engraving and ornamentation services, adding artistic and decorative details to wood, metal, and stone surfaces. Our skilled artisans create intricate designs and patterns, enhancing the aesthetic appeal of furniture, fixtures, and architectural elements.

Wallpaper Fixing Works

Our wallpaper fixing services transform your interiors with precision and attention to detail. We offer a wide selection of wallpaper options and ensure seamless application, from surface preparation to final alignment.

False Ceiling & Light Partitions Installation

We specialize in the installation of false ceilings and light partitions, designed to enhance both the appearance and functionality of interior spaces. Our solutions include installing suspended ceilings, light partition walls, and integrating lighting fixtures, creating a clean and polished look.
